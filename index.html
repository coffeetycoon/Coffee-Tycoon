☕ Welcome to Coffee Tycoon!

How to Play:

Build your coffee empire from the ground up! Click the coffee cup to brew coffee manually, then purchase items from the Shop to automate your production.

Quick Keys:

1 Brew House - Manual brewing
2 Shop - Purchase production items
3 Upgrades - Permanent improvements
4 Prestige - Reset for bonuses
5 Achievements - Track progress

Goal:

Earn coffee, unlock upgrades progressively, and reach 1 million coffee to prestige for permanent Golden Coffee bonuses!

Coffee Tycoon v1.6.3

🎉 What's New in v1.6.3

  • 💰 Rebalanced Economy: Shop items now follow power-of-10 progression for better pacing
  • 🔧 Improved Upgrades Tab: Wider upgrade packs (450px) and better text layout
  • ✨ UI Polish: Fixed "OWNED" indicator overflow and centered empty state message

🎮 Gameplay Tips

  • Focus on one shop item at a time to unlock its upgrade series
  • Upgrade costs scale ×10 per tier - plan your purchases carefully
  • Global upgrades affect all items - save up for huge boosts!
  • Check locked upgrades to see what milestones to reach next
